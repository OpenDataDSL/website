EEX
============================================================

EEX European Energy Exchange

## Overview

![](/img/data/eex.png)

European Energy Exchange (EEX) is the leading energy exchange in Europe founded in 2002.

The European Energy Exchange (EEX) is the leading energy exchange in Europe which develops, operates and connects secure, liquid and transparent markets for energy and related products. As part of EEX Group, a group of companies serving international commodity markets, EEX offers contracts on Power and Emission Allowances as well as Freight and Agricultural Products.
